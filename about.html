About us

    Weather forecasting is the science of predicting the weather based on physical principles as well as a variety of statistical and empirical methods. Weather forecasting entails making predictions about changes on the Earth's surface caused by atmospheric conditions. The purpose of the project is to provide information that people and organizations can use to reduce weather- related losses and improve societal benefits such as life and property protection, public health and safety, and economic prosperity and quality of life.

Real-Time Weather Forecast

Provides accurate weather forecast in real-time, this includes temperature, weather status ultraviolet index, air pressure, wind status, visbility, humidity, precipitation, predicted sunrise and sunset.

Weather Prediction

Provides accurate daily weather forecast for you to arrange your outdoor activities, travel plans or just being updated and prepared for the weather in the days.

Weather Searches History

Automatically saves location searches along with it's weather information such as temperature weather status, wind direction and wind status for you to go back to that forecast later.
